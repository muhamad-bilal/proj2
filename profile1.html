MILES MORALES
Miles Gonzalo Morales is a Brooklyn Visions Academy Student who was bitten by a radioactive spider, gained spider-like abilities, not wanting to be a hero at first, he was forced to took up the mantle of the masked vigilante after the death of his world's Peter Parker. After finding the strength he needed to save the city from the Super-Collider with the help of the Spider-Gang, Miles became the new Brooklyn's one and only Spider-Man.
