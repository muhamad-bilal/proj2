HOBART BROWN
Hobart "Hobie" Brown was a teenager who after being gifted with spider-like powers and abilities became the masked vigilante known as Spider-Man as a way to fight against the anarchy of London. Fighting against the corrupted government of his city, Spider-Man manage to have a lot of enemies and allies equally with his rebel and intriguing personality, along with his looks he managing to being nicknamed by the city, his villains and most of his allies as Spider-Punk.
